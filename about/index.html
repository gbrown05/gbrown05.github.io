About

Hi, I’m George. I was born and raised in Palo Alto, CA, and I’m currently a senior at Tufts University, where I study cognitive science and computer science. I conduct research with Megan Strait and Matthias Scheutz in the Human-Robot Interaction (HRI) lab at Tufts.

Here in Boston, I’m also a writing tutor and I enjoy playing club baseball in the NECBA. In high school, I was the editor-in-chief of a sports magazine and started its website in 2011. Some of my current interests include cognitive science, HRI, brain-computer interfaces, computer science, product design, and law. I’m also fond of baseball, backpacking, traveling (especially in Latin America – I worked in Costa Rica this summer), and rooting for the San Francisco Giants.

As a college senior, one of my favorite things to do is meet new, interesting people and learn from them. If you’d like to get in touch, please don’t hesistate to contact me.
