Links


Links to a few of the projects I've worked on over the past few years.

Supreme Court Visualizations, April 2015

I worked with Skyler Tom on visualizing Supreme Court data in a way that allows legal scholars to explore. This was a project for my visual analytics class at Tufts.


Visualizing Tanzania, February 14-15, 2015

I worked on a team of four at Tufts IDHack for international development to visualize a dataset from the World Bank in order to help leaders make decisions as Tanzania undergoes a rapid population boom. We won first place in the "Best Use of Data" category.
